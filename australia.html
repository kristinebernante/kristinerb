There's nothing like Australia







My Chronicles to the Land Down Under

Weeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeee!
I had the great opportunity to visit Australia last 2014 because my friend's mom lives there. It's really nice to have rich and nice friends who could bring you along to wonderful places. I wish I knew how to tag her name here, oh well..

Anyway, as expected, our time in Aus was a blast! Did I already mention I have free accommodation and free meals for like almost 2 weeks? Yes! Ganyan kakapal ang mukha ko hahahaha. Ambait lang ng friend ko and ng mom nya. One of their relatives is extra kind; they brought us to Canberra area pa. I'm so blessed talaga.

We went there on their winter time, July..kaloka. Nakakaitim ang sikat ng araw pero nanginginig ako sa lamig. We went to popular tourist places, of course..there was the mandatory zoo visit where you can play with Koala, Kangaroo and see tazmanian devil LOL.

I've placed some links of the place we have visited. Share your Australia experience with me as well. Malay natin, makabalik someday. ^_^
